FAQ Updated & Extended

Is the game free to play?
Yes!
Royal Candy Mania is completely free with optional in-app purchases.
Can I play offline?
Absolutely. All main levels are available offline.
Is this game suitable for kids?
Yes,
it’s family-friendly with no inappropriate content or aggressive ads.
Do levels get harder?
Yes!
Early levels are easy and relaxing, but later ones introduce challenging mechanics like blockers and limited moves.
Are there ads?
Ads
are optional and mostly used to gain bonus boosters. No interrupting ads during gameplay
How often are new levels added?
Every
2–3 weeks, with seasonal updates and bonus regions.
Can I sync progress across devices?
Yes
— connect your game to Google Play to sync and back up your progress.
Is there a leaderboard or social play?
Yes!
Weekly events include leaderboards and special score challenges.
I’m stuck — what do I do?
Try
combining power-ups, revisit previous levels for practice, or spin the daily wheel for helpful items.
