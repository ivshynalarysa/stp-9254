Strategy Tips

Pro Moves for Future Candy Royals

  • Always look for 4 or 5-tile matches

  • Combine boosters when possible (e.g., Bomb + Lollipop)

  • Focus on the level’s main goal — not just clearing tiles

  • Use Color Crush when you need a big board reset

  • Save power — ups for sticky levels

No level is impossible — just sweetly tactical.
